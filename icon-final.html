Next steps to use this icon

  • Export the SVG above as a 1024×1024 PNG — open this page, right-click the large icon, "Save image as" — or use a tool like Figma/Sketch to export at exact pixel size
  • In Xcode: open Runner → Assets.xcassets → AppIcon → drag the 1024×1024 PNG into the App Store slot. Xcode auto-generates all other sizes
  • Alternatively: use MakeAppIcon.com — upload 1024×1024, it generates all iOS sizes at once
  • Colours to use in-app: background #060c14 (already close to your dark theme), accent #7eb8d4 for celeste-blue highlights
