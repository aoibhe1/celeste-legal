03 — Luna Iterations · New

Luna — Shape & Colour Variations

Keeping the mauve & slate palette. Exploring what changes when you shift the crescent's weight, angle, proportion, and supporting elements.

New

Thin Crescent

Barely a sliver — waxing crescent phase. Delicate, minimal, almost disappearing.

New

Fat Crescent

Almost a gibbous moon — heavier, more grounded. Confident and full.

New

Tilted

Crescent rotated 30° — opening faces up, like a bowl catching starlight.

New

Facing Left

Waning crescent — mirrored. A different energy: introspective, releasing.

New

Star Inside

A 4-point star rests inside the crescent curve — the moon cradling a star.

New

Double Crescent

Two nested crescents — inner solid, outer a ghost. Depth and echo.

New

Terracotta

Warm earth tone — grounded, natural, seasonal. Very different from the blue-slate family.

New

Silver & Navy

Cool silver-white on deep navy. Classic, minimal, almost monochrome. Works perfectly next to TikTok.

New

Blush & Plum

Soft blush on deep plum. The most delicately feminine of all — lightest touch.

New

Crescent + Orbit

Mauve crescent with an ivory companion in orbit above it. Moon and satellite.

New

Sage & Forest

Muted sage green on dark. Earthy, grounded, healing energy. Unexpected and fresh.

New

Celeste Blue

The literal colour "celeste" — sky blue in Italian. The name and the icon united.
