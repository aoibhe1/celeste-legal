Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2025  ·  Last updated: March 2025

Celeste ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it. By using the Celeste app you agree to this policy.

1. Who we are

Celeste is operated by The Girl Club / Nexwork (sole trader, Poland). If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at ewa@nexwork.io.

2. What data we collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide Celeste's features:

  • Account data — email address and password (or Apple/Google sign-in token). Required to create and secure your account.
  • Birth data — date of birth, time of birth (optional), birth city and coordinates. Used to calculate your astrological profile. Stored securely and never shared.
  • Profile data — display name, language preference, experience points, streak count.
  • Check-in data — daily emotion you select. Used to personalise your experience and track your wellbeing streak.
  • Usage analytics — anonymised events (e.g. "opened horoscope", "completed onboarding"). Collected via PostHog (EU servers). No personally identifiable information is included in analytics events.
  • Crash reports — anonymised error and crash data collected via Sentry (EU servers) to help us fix bugs.

We do not collect your location in real time, access your contacts, microphone, or camera, or track you across other apps.

3. How we use your data

  • To provide and personalise the app (horoscopes, AI guidance, moon phase, wellbeing features)
  • To maintain your account and subscription
  • To improve the app through anonymised analytics and crash reports
  • To send transactional emails (password reset, account notifications) — never marketing without consent

4. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract — processing necessary to provide the service you signed up for
  • Legitimate interests — anonymised analytics and crash reporting to improve the app
  • Consent — where required (e.g. optional marketing communications)

5. Data storage and security

Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL database hosted in EU-West). All data is encrypted at rest and in transit (TLS 1.2+). Access is restricted by row-level security policies — you can only read your own data. Anonymised analytics and crash data are processed on EU servers (PostHog Frankfurt, Sentry Frankfurt).

6. Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We share it only with the services needed to run Celeste:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (privacy policy)
  • Anthropic — AI responses generated via Claude API. Only the text of your query is sent; no personal identifiers (privacy policy)
  • PostHog — anonymised product analytics, EU servers (privacy policy)
  • Sentry — anonymised crash reporting, EU servers (privacy policy)
  • RevenueCat — subscription management (privacy policy) — when subscriptions are enabled
  • Apple / Google — if you sign in with Apple or Google, their authentication services process your sign-in

7. Your rights (GDPR)

As a user in the EU/EEA you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction — ask us to limit processing in certain circumstances
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, email ewa@nexwork.io. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated analytics data may be retained longer as it cannot be linked back to you.

9. Children

Celeste is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Significant changes will be notified in the app. The "last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the current version.
